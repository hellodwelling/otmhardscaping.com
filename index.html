About Us

Luis Flores started out as a delivery driver for a paver company. Since then, he started OTM Hardscape where he and his team delivers quality home remodeling services with love and passion. OTM Hardscape is a licensed and insured hardscaping and home remodeling company, locally owned and operated in Houston for the past 10 years. Built from a highly educated and trained team of professionals with extensive knowledge of landscape architecture, OTM Hardscape provides full services for hardscaping, landscaping, excavation, pergolas, fire pits, retaining walls, and more. Servicing the Houston and surrounding areas with residential and commercial experience. OTM Hardscape is proud to bring Houston the best outdoor living areas and other landscaping services at the best price. Call us today for a free estimate on any work you'd like done.
